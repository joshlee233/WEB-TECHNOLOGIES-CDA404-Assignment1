Privacy policy for Josh Lee's portfolio


Name of website owner/data processor: Joshua Lee

Contact email address: contact@joshual.ee


On the website the data that will be collected via the contact form is as below:


Full name - This will be obtained by the user entering their full name

Email address - This will be obtained by the user entering their email address


Your data will be used for the purpose of getting in contact and replying to any messages sent to the website owner via the contact form on the website.


Your personal data will not be shared with anyone other than the website owner.


We will not directly store your data but your data will be received by our mail server and held in our mailing list. This data will be stored on a server in the UK (United Kingdom).


Your data will only be held for as long as necessary, this means that we will not use or hold your personal data after a year of inactivity.


Inactivity implies that no contact has been made from either party (website owner or contactee) within a year after the last email was sent.



Your individual rights from the GDPR website are below:

  1. The right to be informed
  2. The right of access
  3. The right to rectification
  4. The right to erasure
  5. The right to restrict processing
  6. The right to data portability
  7. The right to object
  8. Rights in relation to automated decision making and profiling.
